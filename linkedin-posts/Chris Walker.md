## Post #1 

The human skills most rapidly declining in value:

-Memorizing information
-Hustle, grinding, and overworking
-Finding and following “best practices”
-Execution without clarity
-Collecting credentials to signal worth
-Incremental optimization

These skills were most rewarded when information was scarce, systems were stable, and change was slow.

__

The human skills that will be most valuable in the future:

-Clarity
-Self-Trust
-Discernment
-Emotional Sovereignty
-Intuition
-Creativity
-Connection
-Adaptability
-Autonomy 

These are the skills that matter most when information is infinite, systems are unstable, and change is fast and constant.

## Post #2 

The top 10 signs you work in a low-frequency company:

1. Constant artificial urgency and pressure. 
2. Overworking and grinding is the norm. 
3. Telling the truth or speaking your mind feels risky. 
4. External metrics of clicks, impressions, leads, or sales drive every decision. 
5. You spend more time talking about “competitors” than customers.
6. You have a constant feeling of falling behind and you can’t keep up. 
7. No matter how much you accomplish, it’s just never enough. 
8. Creativity and innovation is talked about but never actually happens. 
9. Zoom meetings all day. Every meeting feels tight and transactional. 
10. Constant comparison to other people’s funding, growth, or metrics. 

The downstream consequences of this are super predictable:

People feel ongoing stress and anxiety. 
Poor sleep quality. 
Wake up already feeling drained. 
Decision quality and speed degrades. 
Constant overthinking and second-guessing. 
Risk avoidance to stay safe. 

A big group of smart, talented people producing mediocre work because the frequency of the company is systemically suppressing their talents and capacity. 

The common "corporate culture" suppresses all the things that are now most economically valuable - creativity, clarity, vision, adaptability, deep human connection. 

In the AI Era, the Frequency of your company is now the most powerful and sustainable moat.
