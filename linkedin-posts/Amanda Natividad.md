## Post #1 

If you're winning deals on features and pricing, I have bad news.

You've already lost.

In most categories everyone has the same features at roughly the same price, and whatever edge you have gets copied by next quarter.

So once a buyer confirms you fit the budget and check the boxes, what makes them pick you over the other two contenders?

Not another spec.

It's whether your messaging makes them feel understood — whether you clearly know what they've tried before, why it let them down, and what's keeping them up at night.

That's the case Talia Wolf makes on this week's episode of Zero Click Marketing. She always describes a comparison page she rebuilt — making it longer, more detailed, more specific — that drove 54% more free trials.

Her bigger claim might be the one that sticks with you: B2B is more emotional than B2C.

Listen wherever you get your podcasts!
